Edward Su

Edward is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science studying real-time stylised rendering techniques. He holds a MRes in Computer Science and a B.Sc. in Games Development from the University of Technology Sydney.

Edward has conducted research in the field of cooperative AI where he has worked with clustering and reinforcement learning techniques to develop AI capable of cooperating with human partners. This work can be found in IEEE Computational Intelligence Magazine under the title Better Understanding of Humans for Cooperative AI through Clustering.

Edward has previously worked as a game designer and devops engineer at local games studios around Sydney. Before this, he formed the Caps Collective with other university graduates and published the game Fantasy Town Regional Manager which received numerous awards upon its release on Steam.
